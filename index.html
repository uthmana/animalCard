Dove

Doves are amazing animals. Their capacity to find their way home over hundreds, even thousands of kilometres is unrivalled in the animal kingdom

  • Scientific Name: Columbidae.
  • Average Length: 31cm
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years.
  • Habitat: urban areas.

There are more than 300 species in the family.They usually make nests of sticks, and their two white eggs are incubated by both the male and the female parent. Doves feed on seeds, fruit and plants.
